    Seeking founding officers and interested members for fall 2025…

    The Butler University Institute for Operations Research and Management Science (INFORMS) prepares members to be servant leaders and skilled decision facilitators who contribute to a better world through data-driven insight and action. In alignment with INFORMS’ mission, we seek to advance the science and practice of decision making by collaborating with campus organizations, industry partners, and university events. Together, we aim to build a vibrant community that applies analytics and technology to solve problems, create value, and promote growth and excellence.

    What we do:
    • Guest speakers & hands-on workshops
    • Data competitions
    • Community engagement
    • Certification resources & prep - AWS, Azure, CAP-E
    • Faculty-mentored research projects (for course credit or pro-bono)
    • Professional conference trips and poster presentations

    Meetings:
    We meet the first Wednesday every month. Refer to the schedule for time and location. FREE pizza served at meetings.

    Membership Dues & Benefits:
    • Dues: First semester free; $20 / semester thereafter
    • Access to INFORMS Career Connect services
    • FREE subscriptions to DataCamp to access all courses
    • FREE access to AWS Academy courses
    • Support developing an analytics portfolio for your career search
    • Opportunities to present at INFORMS conference events
    • Opportunities to publish in ORMS Tomorrow student magazine
    • Discounts on Certified Analytics Professional (CAP) designations
    • Discounts on Associate Certified Analytics Professional (aCAP) Pathway designation
    • Service Award recognitions

    Great for the following majors/minors, but OPEN TO ALL:
    • Actuarial Science / Math / Statistics / Physics
    • Business Technology and Analytics
    • Computer Science / Data Science
    • Economics
    • Risk Management & Insurance
    • Supply Chain Analytics
