    Seeking founding officers and interested members for fall 2025…

    The Butler University Institute for Operations Research and Management Science (INFORMS) Student Chapter helps prepare students to become better decision facilitators and servant leaders in their future data-driven careers. We seek opportunites to collaborate and partner with other campus clubs & organizations, industry partners, and campus events to build a tech community committed to growth and excellence.

    What we do:
    • Guest speakers & hands-on workshops
    • Data competitions
    • Community engagement
    • Certification prep (AWS, Azure, INFORMS CAP-E)
    • Faculty-mentored research projects (for course credit or pro-brono)
    • Professional conference trips

    Meetings:
    We meet the first and third Wednesday every month at 12:15pm. A consistent Location is TBD. FREE pizza served at meetings.

    Membership Dues & Benefits:
    • Dues: First semester free; $20 / semester thereafter
    • Access to INFORMS Career Connect services
    • FREE subscriptions to DataCamp to access all courses
    • FREE access to AWS Academy courses
    • Discounts to INFORMS regional and national events
    • Opportunities to publish in ORMS Tomorrow student magazine
    • Discounts on Certified Analytics Professional (CAP) designations

    Great for the following majors/minors, but open to all
    Actuarial Science, Business Technology and Analytics, Computer Science, Data Science, Math, Physics, Supply Chain, Statistics
