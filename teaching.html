    Teaching Philosophy

    As an educator, I strive to nurture in my students a deep appreciation for the transformative growth that comes from following a thoughtful, structured process and from working together with empathy and mutual respect. I find great joy in teaching predictive and prescriptive analytics, and in guiding students to integrate these disciplines using the latest in cloud-based and AI technologies to create meaningful impact. All my courses are designed to be hands-on, where every student has tangible evidence to showcase their learning.
